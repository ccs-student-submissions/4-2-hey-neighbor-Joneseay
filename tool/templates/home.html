About Us

Tool Shed Share is a community of people who believe in doing things for yourself. Sometimes this means that you are going to need tools. Sometimes you only need the tool for this one job. This means you don't want to go out and spend a ton of money on a tool you only use once. So we here at Tool Shed Share have banded together to facilitate that. We connect tool owners with people who need their tools, so that we can all get things done.
